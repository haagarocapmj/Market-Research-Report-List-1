<p><p>The amniotic product market comprises three main types: cryopreserved amniotic membranes, dehydrated amniotic membranes, and amniotic suspensions. Cryopreserved membranes are preserved at low temperatures to maintain their bioactivity for surgical applications. Dehydrated membranes, which have a longer shelf life, are used in wound healing and tissue regeneration. Amniotic suspensions contain the liquid component of amniotic fluid, rich in growth factors, and are utilized in various medical fields, including orthopedics and dermatology, for their regenerative properties.</p></p>
