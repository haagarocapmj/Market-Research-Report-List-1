<p><p>The weather strip market comprises various materials, each offering unique benefits for sealing and insulation. EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) is known for its excellent weather resistance and durability, making it ideal for outdoor applications. TPE (Thermoplastic Elastomer) provides flexibility and easy processing, suitable for a range of applications. TPO (Thermoplastic Polyolefin) combines strength and UV resistance, often used in roof membranes. Other materials like PVC and silicone are also prevalent, catering to specific requirements in diverse industries.</p></p>
