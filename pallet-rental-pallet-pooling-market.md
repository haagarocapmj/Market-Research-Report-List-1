<p><p>Pallet rental and pallet pooling are logistics solutions aimed at optimizing supply chain efficiency through the shared use of pallets. Pallet rental involves leasing pallets for short-term use, allowing businesses to avoid the costs associated with purchasing and maintaining their own pallet inventory. On the other hand, pallet pooling refers to a shared system where multiple users access a common pool of pallets, which are managed and maintained by a pooling company. This system enhances resource utilization and reduces waste, contributing to cost savings and sustainability.</p><p>The Pallet Rental & Pallet Pooling Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.2% during the forecast period. Technological advancements, such as tracking solutions and automation in pallet management, further enhance the attractiveness of pallet rental and pooling services.
