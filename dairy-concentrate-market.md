<p><p>The dairy concentrate market is segmented into two primary types: powder and paste. Powdered dairy concentrates, such as milk powder and whey powder, are dehydrated forms that offer long shelf life and ease of storage, making them ideal for various food applications. Paste dairy concentrates, including concentrated milk and cream, retain a higher moisture content, providing a rich flavor and texture for use in sauces, desserts, and beverages. Both forms are integral to the food industry, enhancing nutritional value and convenience.</p></p>
