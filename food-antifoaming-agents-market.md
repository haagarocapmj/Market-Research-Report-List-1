<p><ul><li>Water-Based</li><li>Silicone-Based</li><li>Oil-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Food antifoaming agents are categorized into three main types: water-based, silicone-based, and oil-based agents. Water-based antifoams are typically used in aqueous solutions to control foam in food processing, while silicone-based agents are effective across a variety of temperatures and are known for their strong foam suppression properties. Oil-based antifoams are derived from vegetable oils and are useful in applications where oil compatibility is essential. Each type serves to enhance food production efficiency by minimizing undesirable foam formation.</p></p>
