<p><p>Restaurant catering systems are categorized into cloud-based and on-premises markets. Cloud-based systems operate over the internet, offering flexibility and accessibility from any location, ideal for businesses needing remote access and real-time updates. They typically involve subscription pricing. The choice between these systems depends on a restaurant's specific operational needs and budget constraints.</p></p>
