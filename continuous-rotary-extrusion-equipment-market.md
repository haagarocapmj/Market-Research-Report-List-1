<p><p>The Continuous Rotary Extrusion Equipment market is primarily segmented into two types: Curvilinear Type and Radial Type Continuous Extrusion Machines. Curvilinear Type machines utilize a curved path in their design to enhance material flow and minimize stresses during the extrusion process, making them suitable for complex profiles. In contrast, Radial Type machines feature a radial arrangement that allows for uniform material distribution, ideal for producing components with consistent cross-sections. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
